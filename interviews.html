Interview Protocol

A Guide to Interview Prep and Framework

The job interview is one of the most crucial steps in your career search process. It is the organization's opportunity to determine mutual fit. There are three critical questions, often referred to as "the big three," that you need to be able to address in preparation:

  • WHY YOU: Articulating a response to "Tell Me About Yourself."
  • WHY THIS POSITION? Understanding the role demands through the job description and online research.
  • WHY THIS ORGANIZATION? Understanding the organization and the industry.

  • Practical preparation techniques: Concrete methods for preparing and practicing for medical school interviews, drawing from the experience of someone who successfully navigated the process and then helped evaluate other candidates.
